Before they could say more than “fine,” the dungeon door opened and
Slughorn’s belly preceded him out of the door. As they filed into the
room, his great walrus mustache curved above his beaming mouth, and he
greeted Harry and Zabini with particular enthusiasm. The dungeon was,
most unusually, already full of vapors and odd smells. Harry, Ron, and
Hermione sniffed interestedly as they passed large, bubbling
cauldrons. The four Slytherins took a table together, as did the four
Ravenclaws. This left Harry, Ron, and Hermione to share a table with
Ernie. They chose the one nearest a gold-colored cauldron that was
emitting one of the most seductive scents Harry had ever inhaled:
