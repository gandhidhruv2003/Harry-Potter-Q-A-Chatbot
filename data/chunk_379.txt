minutes later, four broomsticks came swooping down out of the
darkness. Charlie’s friends were a cheery lot. They showed Harry and
Hermione the harness they’d rigged up, so they could suspend Norbert
between them. They all helped buckle Norbert safely into it and then
Harry and Hermione shook hands with the others and thanked them very
