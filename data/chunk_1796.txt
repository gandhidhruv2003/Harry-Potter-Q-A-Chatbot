The atmosphere inside number four, Privet Drive was extremely tense.
The imminent arrival at their house of an assortment of wizards was
making the Dursleys uptight and irritable. Uncle Vernon had looked
downright alarmed when Harry informed him that the Weasleys would be
arriving at five o’clock the very next day. “I hope you told them to
dress properly, these people,” he snarled at once. “I’ve seen the sort
of stuff your lot wear. They’d better have the decency to put on
normal clothes, that’s all.” Harry felt a slight sense of foreboding.
