reason had taken issue with the order to cut up the sopophorous bean
and had written in the alternative instruction: Crush with flat side
of silver dagger, releases juice better than cutting. “Sir, I think
you knew my grandfather, Abraxas Malfoy?” Harry looked up; Slughorn
