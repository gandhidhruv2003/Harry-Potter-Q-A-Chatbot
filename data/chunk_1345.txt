steps, he lost count as he climbed, watching his feet. . . . Then,
without warning, his head hit something hard. It seemed to be a
trapdoor. Harry stood there, massaging the top of his head, listening.
He couldn’t hear any sounds above him. Very slowly, he pushed the
trapdoor open and peered over the edge. He was in a cellar, which was
full of wooden crates and boxes. Harry climbed out of the trapdoor and
