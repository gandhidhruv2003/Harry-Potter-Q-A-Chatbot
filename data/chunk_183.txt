Professor McGonagall had returned. One by one, the ghosts floated away
through the opposite wall. “Now, form a line,” Professor McGonagall
told the first years, “and follow me.” Feeling oddly as though his
legs had turned to lead, Harry got into line behind a boy with sandy
hair, with Ron behind him, and they walked out of the chamber, back
across the hall, and through a pair of double doors into the Great
Hall. Harry had never even imagined such a strange and splendid place.
