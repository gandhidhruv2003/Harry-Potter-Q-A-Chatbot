long.” “You do it, then, if you’re so clever,” Ron snarled. Hermione
rolled up the sleeves of her gown, flicked her wand, and said,
“Wingardium Leviosa!” Their feather rose off the desk and hovered
about four feet above their heads. “Oh, well done!”
