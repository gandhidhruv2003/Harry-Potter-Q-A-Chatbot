following morning, it was bleeding again. He did not complain during
the evening’s detention; he was determined not to give Umbridge the
satisfaction; over and over again he wrote I must not tell lies and
not a sound escaped his lips, though the cut deepened with every
letter. The very worst part of this second week’s worth of detentions
