crossing the world, as friends do the seas; they live in one another
still. For they must needs be present, that love and live in that
which is omnipresent. In this divine glass, they see face to face; and
their converse is free, as well as pure. This is the comfort of
friends, that though they may be said to die, yet their friendship and
society are, in the best sense, ever present, because immortal.
