sprinted across the yard and dived headlong into one of the Wellington
boots that lay scattered around the door. Harry could hear the gnome
giggling madly as Crookshanks inserted a paw into the boot, trying to
reach it. Meanwhile, a very loud crashing noise was coming from the
other side of the house. The source of the commotion was revealed as
they entered the garden, and saw that Bill and Charlie both had their
wands out, and were making two battered old tables fly high above the
lawn, smashing into each other, each attempting to knock the other’s
