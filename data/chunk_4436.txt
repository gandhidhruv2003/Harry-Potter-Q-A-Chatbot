“It’s through here —” He glanced around at them all. They had their

the place: high as a church and full of nothing but towering shelves

issuing from more candle brackets set at intervals along the shelves.
Like those in the circular room behind them, their flames were burning
blue. The room was very cold. Harry edged forward and peered down one
of the shadowy aisles between two rows of shelves. He could not hear
