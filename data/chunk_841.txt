The Slytherin common room was a long, low underground room with rough
stone walls and ceiling from which round, greenish lamps were hanging
on chains. A fire was crackling under an elaborately carved
mantelpiece ahead of them, and several Slytherins were silhouetted
around it in high-backed chairs. “Wait here,” said Malfoy to Harry and
Ron, motioning them to a pair of empty chairs set back from the fire.
