Harry went first, his wand illuminated, expecting at any moment to

fixed upon the swinging beam of the wand held in his fist. At last the
tunnel began to slope upward and Harry saw a sliver of light ahead.
Hermione tugged at his ankle. “The Cloak!” she whispered. “Put the
Cloak on!” He groped behind him and she forced the bundle of slippery
