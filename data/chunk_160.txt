woman eleven silver Sickles and seven bronze Knuts. Ron stared as
Harry brought it all back in to the compartment and tipped it onto an
empty seat. “Hungry, are you?” “Starving,” said Harry, taking a large
bite out of a pumpkin pasty. Ron had taken out a lumpy package and
