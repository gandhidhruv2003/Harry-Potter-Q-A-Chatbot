Mr. Weasley strolled toward the barrier between platforms nine and
ten, pushing Harry’s trolley and apparently very interested in the
InterCity 125 that had just arrived at platform nine. With a
meaningful look at Harry, he leaned casually against the barrier.
Harry imitated him. In a moment, they had fallen sideways through the
solid metal onto platform nine and three-quarters and looked up to see
the Hogwarts Express, a scarlet steam engine, puffing smoke over a
platform packed with witches and wizards seeing their children onto
the train. Percy and Ginny suddenly appeared behind Harry. They were
panting and had apparently taken the barrier at a run. “Ah, there’s
Penelope!” said Percy, smoothing his hair and going pink again. Ginny
