second later it had shuddered to a halt on “home” with the others, and
they heard him calling from the kitchen. “Coming, Arthur!” called Mrs.
Weasley, hurrying out of the room. A few moments later, Mr. Weasley
came into the warm living room carrying his dinner on a tray. He
looked completely exhausted. “Well, the fat’s really in the fire now,”
he told Mrs. Weasley as he sat down in an armchair near the hearth and
toyed unenthusiastically with his somewhat shriveled cauliflower.
