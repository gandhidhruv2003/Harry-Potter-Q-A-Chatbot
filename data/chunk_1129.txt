“Scabbers!” Ron shouted, racing out of the shop after him; Harry
followed. It took them nearly ten minutes to catch Scabbers, who had
taken refuge under a wastepaper bin outside Quality Quidditch
Supplies. Ron stuffed the trembling rat back into his pocket and
straightened up, massaging his head. “What was that?” “It was either a
very big cat or quite a small tiger,” said Harry. “Where’s Hermione?”
“Probably getting her owl —” They made their way back up the crowded
street to the Magical Menagerie. As they reached it, Hermione came
out, but she wasn’t carrying an owl. Her arms were clamped tightly
around the enormous ginger cat. “You bought that monster?” said Ron,
his mouth hanging open. “He’s gorgeous, isn’t he?”
