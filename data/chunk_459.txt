the mirror and tell me what you see.” Harry walked toward him. I must
lie, he thought desperately. I must look and lie about what I see,
that’s all. Quirrell moved close behind him. Harry breathed in the
funny smell that seemed to come from Quirrell’s turban. He closed his
eyes, stepped in front of the mirror, and opened them again. He saw
his reflection, pale and scared-looking at first. But a moment later,
the reflection smiled at him. It put its hand into its pocket and
pulled out a blood-red stone. It winked and put the Stone back in its
pocket — and as it did so, Harry felt something heavy drop into his
real pocket. Somehow — incredibly — he’d gotten the Stone. “Well?”
