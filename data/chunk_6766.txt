they were alive, sentient, intent upon killing them. Now the fire was
mutating, forming a gigantic pack of fiery beasts: Flaming serpents,
chimaeras, and dragons rose and fell and rose again, and the detritus
of centuries on which they were feeding was thrown up in the air into
their fanged mouths, tossed high on clawed feet, before being consumed
