The forest was black and silent. A little way into it they reached a
fork in the earth path, and Harry, Hermione, and Hagrid took the left
path while Malfoy, Neville, and Fang took the right. They walked in
silence, their eyes on the ground. Every now and then a ray of
moonlight through the branches above lit a spot of silver-blue blood
on the fallen leaves. Harry saw that Hagrid looked very worried.
