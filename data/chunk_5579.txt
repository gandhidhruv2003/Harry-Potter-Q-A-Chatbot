water, because Voldemort had planned it so . . . He flung himself over
to the edge of the rock and plunged the goblet into the lake, bringing
it up full to the brim of icy water that did not vanish. “Sir — here!”

Dumbledore’s face. It was the best he could do, for the icy feeling on
his arm not holding the cup was not the lingering chill of the water.
