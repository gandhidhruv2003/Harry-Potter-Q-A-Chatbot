in front of him. Cedric was going to get there first. Cedric was
sprinting as fast as he could toward the cup, and Harry knew he would
never catch up, Cedric was much taller, had much longer legs — Then
Harry saw something immense over a hedge to his left, moving quickly
along a path that intersected with his own; it was moving so fast
Cedric was about to run into it, and Cedric, his eyes on the cup, had
not seen it — “Cedric!” Harry bellowed. “On your left!” Cedric looked
around just in time to hurl himself past the thing and avoid colliding
with it, but in his haste, he tripped.
