He slid a considerable way down what felt like a stone slide, then
landed on cold, damp earth. He stood up, looking around. It was pitch
dark. He held up his wand, muttered, “Lumos!” and saw that he was in a
very narrow, low, earthy passageway. He raised the map, tapped it with
the tip of his wand, and muttered, “Mischief managed!” The map went
blank at once. He folded it carefully, tucked it inside his robes,
then, heart beating fast, both excited and apprehensive, he set off.
