excitedly. “Harry — would I be asking too much — but no, you wouldn’t
want —” “What is it?” said Harry. “Well, this Halloween will be my
five hundredth deathday,” said Nearly Headless Nick, drawing himself
up and looking dignified. “Oh,” said Harry, not sure whether he should
look sorry or happy about this. “Right.” “I’m holding a party down in
one of the roomier dungeons. Friends will be coming from all over the
