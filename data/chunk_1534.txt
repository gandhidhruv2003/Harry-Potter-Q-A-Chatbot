golden ball was held tight in his fist, beating its wings hopelessly
against his fingers. Then Wood was speeding toward him, half-blinded
by tears; he seized Harry around the neck and sobbed unrestrainedly
into his shoulder. Harry felt two large thumps as Fred and George hit
them; then Angelina’s, Alicia’s, and Katie’s voices, “We’ve won the
Cup! We’ve won the Cup!” Tangled together in a many-armed hug, the
Gryffindor team sank, yelling hoarsely, back to earth. Wave upon wave
