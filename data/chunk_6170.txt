Sudden awareness of what he was holding, of what lived behind the
little golden doors, hit Harry as he spoke. Even after all their
efforts to find it, he felt a violent urge to fling the locket from
him. Mastering himself again, he tried to prise the locket apart with
his fingers, then attempted the charm Hermione had used to open
Regulus’s bedroom door. Neither worked. He handed the locket back to
Ron and Hermione, each of whom did their best, but were no more
successful at opening it than he had been. “Can you feel it, though?”
Ron asked in a hushed voice, as he held it tight in his clenched fist.
