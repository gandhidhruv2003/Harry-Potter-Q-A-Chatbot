thickly carpeted corridor. The moment the Minister had passed out of
sight, Harry tugged the Invisibility Cloak out from under his heavy
black cloak, threw it over himself, and set off along the corridor in
the opposite direction. Runcorn was so tall that Harry was forced to
stoop to make sure his big feet were hidden. Panic pulsed in the pit
of his stomach. As he passed gleaming wooden door after gleaming
wooden door, each bearing a small plaque with the owner’s name and
