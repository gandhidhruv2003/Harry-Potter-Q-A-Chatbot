Breakfast was the usual excitable affair next morning; the Slytherins
hissed and booed loudly as every member of the Gryffindor team entered
the Great Hall. Harry glanced at the ceiling and saw a clear, pale
blue sky: a good omen. The Gryffindor table, a solid mass of red and
gold, cheered as Harry and Ron approached. Harry grinned and waved;
Ron grimaced weakly and shook his head. “Cheer up, Ron!” called
