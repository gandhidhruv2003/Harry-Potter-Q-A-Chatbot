 Can’t wait. He folded this note up very small, and with immense

Hedwig. “Feeling up to a long journey?” he asked her. Hedwig hooted in
a dignified sort of a way. “Can you take this to Sirius for me?” he
said, picking up his letter. “Hang on . . . I just want to finish it.”
He unfolded the parchment and hastily added a postscript. If you want
to contact me, I’ll be at my friend Ron Weasley’s for the rest of the
summer. His dad’s got us tickets for the Quidditch World Cup! The
letter finished, he tied it to Hedwig’s leg; she kept unusually still,
as though determined to show him how a real post owl should behave.
