That third night he found his way more quickly than before. He was
walking so fast he knew he was making more noise than was wise, but he
didn’t meet anyone. And there were his mother and father smiling at
him again, and one of his grandfathers nodding happily. Harry sank
down to sit on the floor in front of the mirror. There was nothing to
stop him from staying here all night with his family. Nothing at all.
Except — “So — back again, Harry?” Harry felt as though his insides
