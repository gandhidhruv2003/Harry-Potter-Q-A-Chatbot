“Bubotubers,” Professor Sprout told them briskly. “They need
squeezing. You will collect the pus —” “The what?” said Seamus
Finnigan, sounding revolted. “Pus, Finnigan, pus,” said Professor
Sprout, “and it’s extremely valuable, so don’t waste it. You will
collect the pus, I say, in these bottles. Wear your dragon-hide
gloves; it can do funny things to the skin when undiluted, bubotuber
pus.” Squeezing the bubotubers was disgusting, but oddly satisfying.
