made a habit of throwing stones through the windows of the Riddle

They knew that old Frank’s devotion to the house and grounds amounted
almost to an obsession, and it amused them to see him limping across
the garden, brandishing his stick and yelling croakily at them. Frank,
for his part, believed the boys tormented him because they, like their
parents and grandparents, thought him a murderer. So when Frank awoke
one night in August and saw something very odd up at the old house, he
merely assumed that the boys had gone one step further in their
